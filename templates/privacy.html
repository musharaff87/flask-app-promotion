Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 09, 2025

At Mushtastic Intelligence, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or interact with our services, including our YouTube channel and related applications.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Website Analytics: We use tools like Google Analytics to collect anonymized data such as IP addresses, browser type, device information, pages visited, and session duration to improve our content and user experience.
  • YouTube Data: If you interact with our YouTube channel (@mustastic_intelligence) via our website or apps, we may access public data like comments or subscription status through the YouTube API, with your consent.
  • Contact Information: If you reach out to us (e.g., via email), we collect your name, email address, and message content to respond to your inquiries.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To analyze website performance and optimize content delivery.
  • To manage and enhance our YouTube channel, including uploading videos, updating metadata, and responding to comments via the YouTube API.
  • To communicate with you regarding inquiries or updates about our services.
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information in these cases:

  • Third-Party Services: With providers like Google (for Analytics and YouTube API) under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to users.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies through Google Analytics to track usage patterns. These are small data files stored on your device. You can disable cookies via your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures, such as encryption of sensitive data and secure server hosting, to protect your information. However, no method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as:

  • Accessing or requesting a copy of your data.
  • Requesting deletion of your personal information.
  • Opting out of analytics tracking (via browser settings or tools like “Do Not Track”).

To exercise these rights, contact us at the details below.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website and services may link to external sites (e.g., YouTube). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies separately.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we learn such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated “Last Updated” date. Check back regularly for the latest version.

10. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or to exercise your rights, reach out to us:

  • Email: musharaff87@gmail.com
  • YouTube: @mustastic_intelligence
